Aiming for 30 different plants per week might sound daunting, but it's easier than you might think. Ditch the outdated 5-a-Day - it doesn't account for the variety needed for a flourishing gut. By incorporating a colorful mix of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts, and seeds throughout the week, you're well on your way to reaching this beneficial goal. This diversity feeds the beneficial bacteria in your gut, promoting better overall health, a stronger immune system, improved digestion, and even positive effects on mood.
